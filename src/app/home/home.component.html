About Me

Hello! I am a project manager for Chick-fil-A in Fort Worth, Texas.

I have a passion for people, gaming, problem-solving, and all things that challenge me to think creatively. I am driven by a desire to continuously learn and grow, both personally and professionally. I believe that every challenge presents an opportunity to improve, and I approach each new endeavor with enthusiasm and a willingness to tackle whatever obstacles come my way.
